About the studio

We're the web studio from around the corner.

Neighbor Web Co. exists because great local businesses kept getting stuck with bad websites — overpriced, overcomplicated, or abandoned by whoever built them.

Our story

Big-agency craft. Corner-store care.

We started this studio after watching too many local owners get one of two bad deals: a $10,000 agency website they didn't need, or a $200 template site that embarrassed them on every phone in town.

There's a better middle: thoughtfully designed, hand-built websites at prices a real small business can justify — from people who answer the phone when something needs changing. That's the whole idea. No account managers, no offshore handoffs, no disappearing acts after launch.

We only work with local businesses — shops, restaurants, trades, studios. It keeps us sharp at the one thing that matters in this niche: turning neighbors who find you online into customers who walk through the door.

What we believe

The values behind every build.

No. 01

Plain English, always

You shouldn't need a glossary to buy a website. We explain everything the way we'd explain it to a friend.

No. 02

Results over decoration

A beautiful site that doesn't ring the phone is a failure. Every design choice serves a call, booking, order, or quote.

No. 03

You own everything

Your domain, your content, your site. If you ever leave us, you take it all with you — no hostage situations.

No. 04

Honest prices, in writing

The quote is the price. Anything extra gets discussed and approved before it happens, not invoiced after.

No. 05

We stick around

A website is a relationship, not a transaction. Most of our clients have been with us since their launch day.

No. 06

Local first

We shop at businesses like yours. Helping the neighborhood's storefronts thrive online is the whole point.
